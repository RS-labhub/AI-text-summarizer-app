Super Summarizer App

Welcome to the Super Summarizer App! This app leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence APIs to provide concise summaries of long texts. Whether you have a lengthy article, research paper, or any other text document that you want to summarize quickly, our app can assist you.

Simply paste your text into the text area below and click the "Submit" button.
